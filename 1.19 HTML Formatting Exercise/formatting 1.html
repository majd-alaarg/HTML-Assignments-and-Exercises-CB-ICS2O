The Hidden Meaning of the Bee Movie

While the Bee Movie has, for years, been regarded as a mere children's film, those who are truly immersed in the art of filmaking see it as a masterpiece. The artistic framing of the Bee Movie is engrained within the character himself, Barry B Benson, who finds himself stuck between the world of bees and that of humans. With an intertwined passion, he finds himself in the middle of an apocalyptical event that only he can stop.
